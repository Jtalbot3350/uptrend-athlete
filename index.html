Height from photo

Stand the athlete flat against the wall with the reference mark (e.g. your 7′ line). Shoot from 10–15 ft back, camera level, about chest height. Drag the three lines: FLOOR, REF MARK, TOP OF HEAD.
Mark the wall in inches up to 10′ (your ref mark can be any height — just enter what it is, e.g. 10′0"). Step 1: athlete stands flat-footed, arm straight up — photo, drag FINGERTIP to the fingertips, tap Set standing reach. Step 2: athlete jumps and touches as high as they can — load a video of the jump (slo-mo is best), scrub to the peak, drag FINGERTIP to the hand, tap Set jump touch. Vertical = touch − reach. Recalibrate FLOOR/REF on each new photo or video.
